About Me

Hello! My name is Karla Gonzalez and I'm a senior here at Anaheim High School. I have an older brother who is a junior at UCI and a younger sister who is in the 6th grade and come from a supportive family. I've been apart of various clubs and organizations, including the National Honor Society (NHS) for 3 years, Puente for 3 years, Dual Language Academy (DLA) for 6 years, and Stitch by Stitch for 2 years helping it grow and expand. Some hobbies of mine include, painting, baking, spending time with family and friends, and crocheting. With my love for crocheting, I started a small business selling hand-made crocheted items, and done Market day and Steam-A-Palooza for the last 2 years. I hope to major in Aerospace Engineering and possibly get a minor in computer science at UCI. I am excited to see what the future holds for me and to continue learning and growing as a person.
